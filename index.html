about

amaralynn (Amara Liem) is an electronic artist and pianist based out of Toronto, ON. Using her artful blend of indie, dance, electronica, and classical influences, she conjures ethereal atmospheres and beautiful piano ballads as naturally as high-octane dance floor jams. Inspired by acts like Porter Robinson and The 1975, her music offers a window into a dream world of colourful soundscapes, heartfelt melodies, and captivating rhythms that you won't want to wake up from.
